# Summary Report: “AWS Cloud Day Vietnam - AI Edition 2025”

 **Event Name:** Vietnam Cloud Day 2025  

 **Date:** September 18, 2025  

 **Location:** 2 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City  

 **Role in the Event:** Participant  

## Event Objectives
The **AWS Cloud Day Vietnam - AI Edition 2025** is positioned as a pivotal gathering for the Vietnamese technology and business communities. The event's primary objective is to catalyze the digital transformation of Vietnam's economy by leveraging the symbiotic power of **Cloud Computing** and **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**.

The overarching goals of the event are structured around four strategic pillars:

* **Democratizing Generative AI for Enterprise:** Shifting Generative AI (GenAI) from concept to practical application. Emphasizes transforming general AI models into domain-specific, context-aware solutions supported by a strong data strategy.
* **Bridging Business–IT Gaps:** Especially in Financial Services, focusing on aligning IT and business priorities to enable models like “Ecosystem Banking” and “Embedded Finance.”
* **Accelerating Industry Modernization:** Showcasing roadmaps tailored for sectors such as Retail, Energy, Telco, and Public Services. Includes success cases from **Xanh SM**, **Honda Vietnam**, and **Masterise Group**.
* **Strengthening Security and Resilience:** Promoting a “security by design” approach, embedding security from development through deployment.

## Key Highlights

### Policy & Leadership
* Opening speeches by government and diplomatic leaders demonstrated strong national and international support for Vietnam’s digital transformation.
* Leadership discussions emphasized how talent and culture accelerate cloud adoption.

### Financial Services Innovation
* Banking leaders discussed new digital models and the shift toward customer-centric ecosystems.
* XGenAI was showcased as a real-world implementation of AI delivering improved financial services.

### Cross-Industry Digital Modernization
* Honda Vietnam shared SAP migration insights.
* Media and telco organizations presented transformation frameworks.
* Cloud partners highlighted cost optimization and modernization pathways.

### Data, AI & DevOps
* Experts stressed that data quality is the decisive factor for successful GenAI.
* DevOps practitioners demonstrated automated testing/code generation using AI.

---

## Key Takeaways

### Design Mindset
* Adopt business-first digital strategies.
* Integrate resilience and continuity in all architectures.

### Technical Architecture
* GenAI effectiveness depends on strong data foundations.
* Use hybrid modernization approaches—refactor, replatform, or migrate based on system needs.

### Modernization Strategy
* “Migrate to Operate” as the long-term model.
* Prioritize API-driven ecosystems.

### Practical Applications
1. Conduct a data readiness audit before launching GenAI initiatives.  
2. Pilot AI-assisted DevOps workflows.  
3. Benchmark SAP/VMware modernization paths.  
4. Adopt integrated security practices (“Security at Scale”).
